Deep Logic & Control

Reactions & Functions. The Brain of Automation.

Most software forces you to work in a specific way. Platform adapts to you. The secret lies in our two core automation building blocks: Reactions and Functions. These tools allow you to model the unique "Pulse" of your production floor. They turn static data into active logic.

Reactions

(The Trigger)

This is the "When." You define an event. It could be an incoming order, a machine error via MQTT, or a database change. Platform watches for this event 24/7. When it happens, the system reacts instantly.

Functions

(The Action)

This is the "What." Powered by Lua scripting, Functions are reusable logic blocks that execute tasks. They can calculate material usage, rename files, or update third-party systems.

The Result

You can build complex, event-driven workflows that mirror your exact business needs. No workarounds required.
