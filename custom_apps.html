The Breakthrough

Your Data. Your Rules. Your App.

This is our biggest breakthrough. Most "app builders" are just empty shells. Custom Apps are different because they live directly inside your data ecosystem.

You do not need to build complex APIs or manage databases. Your app has instant, native access to the entire Platform Data Model. If a machine sends a signal via CoConnect, your app knows it instantly. If a user updates a job status in the app, the ERP is updated automatically.

Live Data Access

Display real-time machine speeds, ink levels, or order statuses without writing a single line of backend code.

Secure by Default

You do not need to worry about user logins or permissions. The app inherits the security and user management of Platform.

Trigger Automations

Your app can start complex workflows. One button press in your app can fire a "Reaction" that sends files, emails, and JMF messages simultaneously.

Developer Freedom & AI

Standard Tech. Vibe Coding. No Limits.

We do not force you to learn a proprietary "CoCoCo-Language" to build user interfaces. We use standard web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

This open standard unlocks a superpower. It means you can use AI to build your apps for you. We are happy to enable Vibe Coding. Because AI tools like ChatGPT or Claude understand these standard languages perfectly, you can simply describe what you want, and the AI writes the code.

Prompt Your App
