Two Products. Two Goals.
Solution A

OpenInfra: The Digital Backbone

For your fleet. For your engineers. No UI.

OpenInfra is a specialized, headless connectivity solution designed exclusively for machine vendors. It does not manage print jobs or shop floor logistics. It focuses on one thing only: It connects your entire machine estate to your central cloud.

It speaks the languages that matter to your hardware. OpenInfra operates close to the metal, engineered to handle high-frequency JMF and proprietary signals. It captures the raw heartbeat of your equipment—sensor data, counter pulses, and error codes—and pipes it securely to your backend.

Remote Telemetry

See exactly how your machines are performing in the field in real-time regardless of where they are installed.

Vendor-Specific Tunnel

This is a private connection for your equipment only. It ignores the surrounding noise of the print shop's other vendors.

Proactive Support

Detect hardware faults in your fleet before the customer even calls you.

Which solution fits your strategy?

Choose OpenInfra if:

  • You only need raw data from your machines.
  • You want to improve your service and support capabilities.
  • You do not need a user interface for the end customer.
  • You are focused on JMF and machine protocols.

Choose White Label if:

  • You want to sell software and workflow tools to your customers.
  • You need a modern HMI or dashboard for your machine.
  • You want to unify job data and production planning.
  • You want to strengthen your brand visibility on the shop floor.
