CoCollab: Seamless Outsourcing & Smart Job Routing

Distribute print jobs effortlessly among your network of partners and PSPs, breaking down the barriers between different MIS and ERP systems.

CoCollab redefines collaboration by creating a secure, agnostic data exchange between you, your subsidiaries, partner and trade printers. Instead of being limited by incompatible software, CoCollab ensures all parties work from a unified CoCoCo Platform data set. Whether you are outsourcing specific finishing tasks or routing entire jobs, the transfer is seamless, eliminating manual data entry and communication errors.

True collaboration is bidirectional. CoCollab doesn't just send orders out; it pipes real-time production status back into your dashboard, giving you full visibility as if the external machine were on your own shop floor. With intelligent automation, you can define rules to let CoCollab independently decide which partner receives which job, turning complex logistics into a "set and forget" background process.
